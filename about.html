🎌 About Milk Matsuri

🥛 Our Mission

Milk Matsuri is a fun, Japanese-themed educational podcast that teaches children the amazing benefits of milk through catchy songs, festival vibes, and memorable stories. We believe every child deserves to learn about healthy habits in a joyful, culturally rich way.

🎯 Our Vision

We envision a world where children excitedly reach for their daily glass of milk — not because they're told to, but because they understand its power! Through Japanese festival music (Bon Odori, Taiko, Ghibli-style), we make learning about nutrition an unforgettable adventure.
